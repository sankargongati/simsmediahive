Wildlife Foundation

We can do more than co-exist.

Together, we can protect the wild that protects us.

We rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife that has been abandoned, displaced, or injured — restoring balance between humans and nature.

Approach

Protecting life through compassion, conservation, and community.

RESCUE & REHABILITATION

Our teams respond to calls from villages and towns to rescue wild animals that have strayed from their natural habitats. Each animal receives professional medical care and safe rehabilitation before release.

HABITAT PROTECTION

We work with forest departments and local communities to restore damaged ecosystems and ensure that wildlife has safe, thriving environments to return to.

COMMUNITY AWARENESS

We educate and empower local communities to live harmoniously with wildlife, preventing conflict and spreading awareness about conservation practices.

MONITORING & RESEARCH

Using modern tracking and medical technologies, we monitor rescued animals and study their adaptation patterns to improve future conservation efforts.

Our Work

Every rescue tells a story of hope, compassion, and second chances.

About Us

The Wildlife Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to the protection and welfare of wild animals. Our mission is to rescue, heal, and return displaced or injured wildlife to their natural homes while promoting awareness about human-wildlife coexistence.
